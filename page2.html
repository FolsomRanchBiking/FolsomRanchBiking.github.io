Bike Lane Construction Update
 
 
Pavement has finally been layed down for the extended part of the bike lane recently. This bike lane should connect Conelly Cir to Nightberry Dr. The bike lane is still being constructed as the connecting bike lane to Mangini Pkwy is still being constructed. There is no official date for when the bike lane will be completed, but it should be expected for it be completely finished by the end of 2024, based on the progress seen with the recent extension of work done.
